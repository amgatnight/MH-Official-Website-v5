One Room, Two Acts

Montreal's destination for guests who want dinner and the night that follows.

Yoko Luna was conceived around a conviction that the best nights in a city don't require a venue change — that a room can hold an elevated dinner, a proper DJ set, and bottle service at full energy without any of them feeling compromised. The result is a cinematic space that draws Montreal's most discerning diners early and holds the city's nightlife crowd through closing.

Through Montreal Hospitality, Yoko Luna handles everything from individual dinner reservations to full group takeovers, birthday programming, and corporate evening buyouts. The table is curated, the bottle service is seamless, and the booking team coordinates every detail so the transition from supper club to nightclub feels inevitable rather than improvised.
