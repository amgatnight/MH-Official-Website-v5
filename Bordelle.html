A Room Like No Other

French elegance, Old Port energy, one unforgettable room.

Bord'Elle occupies a singular position in Montreal's nightlife — a supper club and cabaret that takes the Old Port's architectural grandeur and charges it with an after-dark energy that's equal parts refined and alive. The room does dinner and it does late night, and it does both without compromise.

Through Montreal Hospitality, Bord'Elle accommodates everything from intimate date nights and corporate dining to full venue buyouts, birthday celebrations, and guest list programming. The champagne program is the lead act, backed by French-inspired small plates and entertainment that builds through the evening.
