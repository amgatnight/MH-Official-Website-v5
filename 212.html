No Compromises

Montreal's dedicated nightclub for guests who won't settle for less.

212 was built around a single conviction: that Montreal deserves a club without compromise — one with a proper sound system, a real booking program, a lighting rig designed for headline acts, and a VIP operation that doesn't feel like an afterthought. The result is a room that draws both the city's nightlife regulars and international guests who know what they're looking for before they arrive.

Through Montreal Hospitality, 212 handles everything from individual VIP table reservations to group takeovers, birthday programming, and corporate evening buyouts. The guest list is curated, the bottle service is seamless, and the booking team handles coordination so the night runs the way a headline show should.
