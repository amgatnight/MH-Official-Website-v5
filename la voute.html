Built Different

A historic bank vault turned into Montreal's most dramatic nightlife room.

La Voute occupies the original vaulted hall of one of Old Montreal's historic bank buildings — a space whose 40-foot stone ceilings and arched architecture were never designed for nightlife, which is exactly what makes them so extraordinary inside it. The acoustics are perfect. The atmosphere is unmatched. The room announces itself the moment you walk in.

Through Montreal Hospitality, La Voute handles casino nights, headline DJ events, private buyouts, and regular weekend programming. Whether you're coming for a casino night with friends, a bottle table at a headline show, or a full corporate buyout of one of Old Montreal's most storied spaces, La Voute handles every scale of the occasion.
