The Escape

An hour from the city. A world away from the ordinary.

Beach Club sits on the waterfront in Pointe-Calumet — Quebec's premier outdoor venue where the river meets a level of programming you'd expect from a destination festival. Daybeds, cabanas, pools, and a main stage that hosts some of the biggest DJ names in the world, all against the backdrop of the St. Lawrence.

Through Montreal Hospitality, Beach Club offers guest list access, daybed and cabana reservations, bottle service, and full group concierge for anyone who wants the waterfront experience handled properly. Whether it's a day trip for two or a group booking for twenty, the team makes it effortless.
