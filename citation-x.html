Overview

Citation X

The Citation X was the first civil aircraft to achieve Mach 0.92 — making it the fastest business jet ever built. For the time-conscious traveller, nothing else competes. Beyond its extraordinary velocity, a spacious stand-up cabin accommodates up to 12 passengers in genuine comfort. The swept-wing design delivers both record-breaking speed and exceptional ride stability at 51,000 feet.
