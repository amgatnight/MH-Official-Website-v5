A Different Kind Of Bar

A cocktail lounge that knows how to turn into a party.

Hang Bar is built around the moment when a 5 à 7 turns into a full night out. The bar is the centerpiece — long, lit, and always working — surrounded by lounge seating designed for groups that don't want to leave when the music gets louder.

Through Montreal Hospitality, the venue serves both casual after-work crowds and reservation-led nightlife groups. Whether it's a quick cocktail meeting, a birthday round of bottles, or a full table takeover, Hang Bar handles every register of the evening without changing rooms.
